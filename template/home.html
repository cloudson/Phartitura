What means 'phartitura'?

"Phartitura" is inspired on the word "Partitura" . It means a sheet music, the object that a musician uses to play a song.

You want know if your library is up to date. Get on a phartitura that shows it for you.

Phartitura is an application that shows to you the current state of a library on composer.
